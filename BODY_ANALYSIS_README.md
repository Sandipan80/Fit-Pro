1. Navigate to Tools ‚Üí Body Analysis in the navigation menu
2. Enter your body measurements (height, weight, wrist/ankle circumference, etc.)
3. Answer questions about your body characteristics
4. Define your fitness goals and preferences
5. Generate your personalized analysis
